\begin{exercise}

Let $t$ be an angle with $\cos t = -\frac{2}{5}$ that lies in quadrant III.  Compute exact values for the following expressions.
\begin{align*}
\sin t &= \answer{-\frac{\sqrt{21}}{5}}\\
\tan t &= \answer{\frac{\sqrt{21}}{2}}\\
\csc t &= \answer{-\frac{5}{\sqrt{21}}}
\end{align*}

\end{exercise}
