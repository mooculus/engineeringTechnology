  $(0,1)$.

The distance, D, between the point $(0,1)$ and a point on the parabola $(x,x^2)$ is given by


$D(x)=\sqrt{(x-0)^2+(x^2-1)^2}$.
We have to find a global minimum of D on its domain $[0,\infty)$.
  \end{hint}
    \begin{hint}
    We have to find the critical points of $D$.
      \end{hint}
  
  \begin{prompt}
  \begin{align*}
  x &= \answer{1/\sqrt{2}}\\
  y &= \answer{1/2}
  \end{align*}
  \end{prompt}
