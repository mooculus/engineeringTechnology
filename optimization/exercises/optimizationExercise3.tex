Let $L_a(x)$ be the linear approximation to $f$ at $a$.

Select the correct statement below.
\begin{multipleChoice}
\choice{$L_a(b)<f(b)$}
\choice[correct]{$L_a(b)>f(b)$}
\choice{$L_a(a)<f(a)$}
\choice{$L_a(a)>f(a)$}
\choice{None of the above statements are correct.}
\end{multipleChoice}
