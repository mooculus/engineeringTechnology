Let $L_a(x)$ be the linear approximation to $f$ at $x=a$.

Select all correct statements below.
\begin{selectAll}
\choice[correct]{$L_a(b) < f(b)$}
\choice{$L_a(b) > f(b)$}
\choice{$L_a(a) < f(a)$}
\choice{$L_a(a) > f(a)$}
\choice{No answer above is correct.}
\end{selectAll}
