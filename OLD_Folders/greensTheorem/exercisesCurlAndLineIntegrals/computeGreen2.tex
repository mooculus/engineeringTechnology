  \[

  fashion. Compute:
  \[
  \oint_{\partial P} \left( (3y+(x-1)^4)\d x + \left(\frac{\cos(y)}{y}\right)\d y\right)
  \begin{prompt}
    = \answer{-24}
  \end{prompt}
  \]
