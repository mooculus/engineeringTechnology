Recall the parametric formula for a  \link[M\"{o}bius
  strip]{https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mobius_strip}:
\[
\vec{M}(\theta,t) =
\begin{bmatrix}
  (1 + t \cos(\theta/2))\cos(\theta)\\
  (1 + t \cos(\theta/2))\sin(\theta)\\
  t \sin(\theta/2)
\end{bmatrix}
\]
for $0\le \theta< 2\pi$ and $-1\le t\le 1$.
