\begin{exercise}
  Consider $F(x,y) = 2x+y$ and the curve $C$ in the $(x,y)$-plane
  defined by $y=x^2$.  Give a vector-valued function that draws $C$ on
  the surface defined by $F$.
  \[
  \vec{p}(t) = \vector{\answer{t},\answer{t^2},\answer{2t+t^2}}
  \]
\end{exercise}
