\begin{exercise}
Let $r(u) = 7 \sin (u) \left(-5 e^u-\frac{3}{\sqrt[5]{u}}\right)$. Compute:
\[
r'(u)
        \begin{prompt}=7\sin{u}\cdot \left(\answer{\frac{3}{5 u^\frac{6}{5}}-5 e^u}\right) +7\cdot\answer{ \cos (u)}\cdot \left(-5 e^u-\frac{3}{{u}^\frac{1}{5}}\right)\end{prompt}
\]
\end{exercise}
