\begin{exercise}
Let $R(u) = \frac{ 5 u^4+3 \sqrt[3]{u}}{-5 \sqrt[3]{u}}$. First simplify, then compute $R'(u)$.
\[
R(u) = -u^{\answer{11/3}}-\frac{3}{5}\cdot u^{\answer{0}}
\]
\[
R'(u)
\begin{prompt}=\left(\answer{-11/3} \right)\cdot{u}^{\answer{8/3}}+\answer{0}\end{prompt}
\]
\end{exercise}
