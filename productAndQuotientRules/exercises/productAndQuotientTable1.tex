\begin{exercise}

Let $f(x) = e^xg(x)+h(x)^2$

We have the following table of values for $g$ and $h$.

\[
\begin{array}{c|c|c|c|c}
 x & g(x) & g'(x) & h(x) & h'(x)\\ \hline
 1 & 2 & 2 & 6 & -3\\ 
 2 & 5 & 2 & 5 & 2\\
 3 & 4 & 3 & 1 & -1\\ 
 4 & -1 & 0 & 2 & 5\\ 
 5 & 3 & 1 & 4 & 9\\ 
\end{array}
\]

Use this data to compute $f'(3)$.

\begin{prompt}
	$f'(3) = \answer{7e^3-2}$
\end{prompt}

\end{exercise}
