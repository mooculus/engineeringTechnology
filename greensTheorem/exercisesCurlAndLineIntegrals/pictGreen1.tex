          every axis x label/.style={at=(current axis.right of origin),anchor=west},

  Suppose that $\curl\vec{F} = 4$ and that $\int_{C_1}
  \vec{F}\dotp\d\vec{p} = 22$. Estimate:
  $\int_{C_2}\vec{F}\dotp\d\vec{p}$

    \[
    \int_{C_2}\vec{F}\dotp\d\vec{p} \approx \answer{-106}
