\begin{exercise}

Find the indefinite integral of $\frac{x^2+2 x+1}{x^3+3 x^2+3 x}$ with respect to $x$.

\[
\int \frac{x^2+2 x+1}{x^3+3 x^2+3 x}\d x=\answer{\frac{1}{3} \ln|x^2+3 x+3|+\frac{\ln|x|}{3}+C}
\]

\end{exercise}
