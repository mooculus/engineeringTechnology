\begin{exercise}

The region $R$ bounded by $y=\sqrt{x}+2$ and $y=x^2+2$ in the first quadrant is revolved about the line $y=-2$.  Calculate the volume of the solid.

The volume of the solid is $\answer{\frac{89}{30} \pi}$ cubic units.
